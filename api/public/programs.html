Kids Martial Arts

For younger students Martial Arts will become catalysis for their character development serving as a foundation for their focus, listening and self control skills. At Legend Martial Arts we use Martial arts as a tool to teach students discipline and respect for themselves, parents, friends and others around them. The uniqueness of Martial Arts is that it’s an individual sport, where a student is judged solely on performance and not compared to others in class.  However, to succeed in class, students must work together, as a team thus developing teamwork and social skills.

Adult Martial Arts

For Teens and Adults Martial Arts will help improve strength, agility, coordination, weight loss and more. Our system at Legend Martial Arts Academy incorporates the benefits of cross training, interval training, strength training and aerobic training. Because we also believe that a sound body is only part of the equation, we also train to strengthen our minds and spirit. Our adult students tell us that their focus, their attitudes, and overall mental state have improved significantly since joining our program; not to mention that they are having a blast in the process!

Camps

Nerf gun wars, water gun fights, bouncy houses and a ton of other awesome educational activities will allow your kids to brag that they had the best summer ever! In addition, they will also benefit from our World Class Martial Arts and Self Defense Program here at Legend Martial Arts Academy Summer Camp! We cater to boys and girls ages, 5-12 and all experience levels… regardless of whether they are currently a student here or not. We are personally dedicated to helping every single one of our campers to develop their martial arts expertise, their physical fitness and their self-defense skills while enjoying all of the fun activities that our camp provides.

Birthday Parties

We offer to host your child’s Birthday Party with our Black Belt Party. Birthday Party package includes: a fun martial arts lesson, and a performance by your child showing off new skills; a super cool performance by our Demo team; cutting the birthday cake with a Samurai sword. We reserve the studio for 2 hours and provide tables and chairs.
