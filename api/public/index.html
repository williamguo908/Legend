OUR MISSION

We provide quality traditional martial arts training for children and adults. All of our programs incorporate universally accepted life skill principles in martial arts instruction. Students develop individual character through martial arts that is interesting, fun and family centered.
We offer many different programs both for kids and adults. Serving the fine communities of Roseville, Rocklin, Lincoln, Citrus Heights and Antelope.

OUR APPROACH

It’s simple. Everyone is unique individually. Our approach is having a curriculum that is adjustable for every student’s needs and abilities. Our curriculum and classes are age and level appropriate and incorporate not only Taekwondo, but, also, the elements of Judo and Hapkido, making our classes dynamic, fun yet challenging for all ages and levels.
